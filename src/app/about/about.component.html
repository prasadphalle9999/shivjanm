Welcome to Shivjanmbhoomi Sewa Foundation

A nonprofit organization (NPO) is one that is not driven by profit but by dedication to a given cause that is the target of all income beyond what it takes to run the organization. Because of this, NPOs receive tax-exempt status from the federal government, meaning they don't have to pay income tax. Nonprofit organizations are often used for trusts, cooperatives, advocacy, charity, environmental and religious groups. Many, but not all, NPOs have paid staff in management positions; almost all use volunteers.
